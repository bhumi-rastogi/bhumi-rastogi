## 🛒 Blinkit Sales Analysis

Analyzed 5,000 Blinkit transactions to study how delivery delays impact customer satisfaction and revenue in quick-commerce.

### Key Insights:
- 62% of delays happen during evening peak hours
- Delays above 10 minutes sharply increase negative feedback
- Orai identified as a top-revenue but high-delay zone

### Solutions Suggested:
- Peak-hour rider optimization
- Delay compensation coupons
- Customer retention campaigns

## 🏛 Government Tender Procurement Analytics

Built a procurement risk and spending efficiency dashboard using 10,000 public contract records from the City of Chicago.

### Key Insights:
- Top vendors controlled nearly 30% of total spend
- Average approval delay was 1180 days
- High blanket contract volatility across departments

### Solutions Suggested:
- Faster approval workflows
- Vendor diversification
- Better blanket contract governance
